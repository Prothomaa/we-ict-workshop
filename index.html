About the Workshop

The Workshop on Women Empowerment through ICT: Higher Studies, Research and Career 2026 (WE-ICT 2026) aims at providing a forum for researchers, faculty members, industry professionals, and students to exchange ideas, experience, and success stories to encourage, support, connect and motivate Bangladeshi women in computing. The WE-ICT 2026 will be held in Dhaka, Bangladesh, February 24, 2026, organized under the subproject CP#2082: Enhancement of Graduates Studies Facilities for the Department of CSE, BUET of Higher Education Quality Enhancement Project (HEQEP). This workshop features keynote speech, invited talks, and poster presentations.
